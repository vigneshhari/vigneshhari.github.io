ABOUT ISQIP

IEEE Student Quality Improvement Programme (ISQIP) is a week long internship training program from IEEE SB CEC, offering both technical and managerial sessions, for the overall development of the student. ISQIP aims at providing the trained students internships, through an internship drive held by various organizations at the college.

Develop Design Skills

CS ISQIP helps you to polish your designing skills and makes you confident enough to design anything you want.

Develop Programming Skills

CS ISQIP helps you to learn programming in a short span of time. At the end of the day you will be able to program with ease.

Develop Electronics Skills

Stop staying just fascinated by the upcoming technologies, EC ISQIP is here to help you to have your hand in the world of electronic gizmos. Learn everything from basic circuits to hardwares.

Internship Offers

ISQIP provides you internship opportunity to let you see what you gained through the program. It will be a chance for you to work as an intern in an iconic company.
